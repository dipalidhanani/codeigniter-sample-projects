Saskatoon’s newest Room Escape Game

BREAKOUT is a live escape game, designed for groups from 4-8 people. In all our games you will work together to solve puzzles and clues that will eventually lead to your escape from the room. You will have 60 minutes to overcome a series of mind bending puzzles and challenges to complete your mission. No special skills are needed. Although these games are challenging, don’t worry- you’ll have fun whether you escape or not!
